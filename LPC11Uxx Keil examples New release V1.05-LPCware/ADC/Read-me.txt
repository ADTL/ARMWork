Purpose:
A program to implement Analog to  Digital Converter (ADC) example on LPC11Uxx

Running mode:
* Compile, Flash the program and reset.
* Setup UART for 115.2K, 8 data bits, no parity, 1 stop bit
* Vary the Pot on board  (AD0)
